Nairobi to: Tsavo West/Tsavo East - Mombasa Safari. 3 Days

These two animal parks are legendary! Tsavo East and Tsavo West are some of the oldest and definitely the largest combined parks in Kenya. Here you will find Africa’s Big Five, a special rhino sanctuary and beautiful watering spots where great herds of elephants gather. This is the home of the famous Tsavo lions, over 600 species of birds and surprises like monkeys, hippos and crocodiles. The landforms here are immense – great swaths of golden savannah, dark stretches of volcanic lava beds and bright, crystalline springs. We’ll set up your journey for any special requests. So, get ready for your private road and rail adventure through the heart of Tsavo!

Cost: 100 $

Amboseli Park is the second most popular park in Kenya. Tsavo West is one of the most renowned. Just contact us to customize your adventure. And get some rest – it’s going to be a very busy three days!
